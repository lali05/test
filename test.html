Depression Information

Understanding Depression

Depression (major depressive disorder) is a common and serious medical illness that negatively affects how you feel, the way you think and how you act.

To learn more about depression, visit Psychiatry.org

Types of Depression

There are several types of depression, including major depressive disorder, persistent depressive disorder, and seasonal affective disorder.

Symptoms of Depression

Click Me!

Depression can have a range of symptoms, which can vary from person to person. Some common symptoms of depression include:

  • Feeling sad or hopeless
  • Loss of interest in activities
  • Changes in appetite or weight

Treatment Options

Effective treatment options for depression include therapy, medication, and lifestyle changes. It's important to seek professional help for proper diagnosis and guidance.
